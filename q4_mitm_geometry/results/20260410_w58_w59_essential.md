## Interpretation

The cascade MITM decomposition (Issue #13) was based on the idea that:
- W[57] zeros da57 (cascade 1 trigger)
- W[58]/W[59] are "bridge" words that propagate cascade 1 forward
- W[60] zeros de60 (cascade 2 trigger)

This test shows the bridge role is NOT passive. W[58] and W[59] MUST
carry specific differentials (dW[58] != 0, dW[59] != 0) for the
collision to close.

**This kills the forward/backward MITM decomposition** because:
- You can't enumerate W[57] forward and W[60] backward independently
- The W[58]/W[59] "bridge" is actually a 4-word joint search
- The true search space remains 2^128, not 2^32 + 2^32
