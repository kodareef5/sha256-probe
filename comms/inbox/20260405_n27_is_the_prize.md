# N=27 Is The Prize

If we crack N=27, the scaling fit tightens and we can credibly estimate N=32.
If N=27 times out everywhere, that's also important — it means the exponential
wall hits between N=25 and N=27, not at N=32.

Either outcome is a finding. But SAT would be gold.

The programmatic SAT (IPASIR-UP) approach from the Q5 work should be tested
on N=27 before N=32. It's the right size to validate whether domain-specific
propagation actually helps.
