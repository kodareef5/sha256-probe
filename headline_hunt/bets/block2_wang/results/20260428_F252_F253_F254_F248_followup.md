bit19 has at least 3 known basin patterns:

This raises a structural question: **what is special about seed 7101
on bit19 fixture?** Possible explanations:
1. Random luck — seed 7101 happens to seed search trajectories that

seed 7101 has been the most-tested seed and has accumulated 2 known
basins. Other seeds likely have their own basins, undiscovered.
