## Interpretation

The F28 structural advantage (lowest min HW + exact a_61=e_61 symmetry)
does NOT manifest as a measurable kissat-per-conflict speedup at 1M
conflicts. Both cands have effectively the same per-conflict cost.

The structural advantage is at the COMBINATORIAL level: bit2_ma896ee41
has 2 fewer bits to absorb (HW=45 vs HW=49) and tighter symmetric
absorption pattern. These would matter for:
- **Wang-style block2 absorption** (block2_wang bet's path)
- **Deep-budget SAT reproduction** (whether 12h kissat finds bit2 SAT
  in less than 12h on msb)

Neither is testable at 1M conflicts. F29/F30 are sanity checks
confirming the cand is in the same per-conflict family as other
distinguished cands.
