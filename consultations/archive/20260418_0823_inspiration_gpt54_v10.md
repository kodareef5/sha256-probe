### Why derived encoding hurt d4

- SAT likes fewer search vars
- compilers hate extra clause interdependence

Do **not** fully substitute W2.

Introduce offset auxiliaries:

```text
O57 = T1nw1_57 - T1nw2_57 + T2_1_57 - T2_2_57

W2_58 = W1_58 + O58
```

#### Encoder sketch
```python
for r in [57,58,59,60]:
```

Use auxiliaries, not inlined expressions.

That may help both:
